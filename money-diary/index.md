# Money Diary

Money Diary is an offline-first personal expense and income tracker for
iPhone and iPad. It helps you record transactions, organize recurring
payments, use daily templates, analyze spending, and manage multiple
currencies.

Your records stay on your device by default. Optional iCloud Sync,
backup, import, and export features are available with Money Diary Pro.
