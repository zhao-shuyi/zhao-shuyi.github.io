# Money Diary Support

Need help with Money Diary, want to report a problem, or have a suggestion?

Email us at [shuyizhao.support@gmail.com](mailto:shuyizhao.support@gmail.com).

We will do our best to respond as soon as reasonably possible. Please do not include transaction details, financial records, backup files, or other sensitive information in your message.

## When Reporting a Problem

Please include:

- A brief description of what happened and what you expected to happen.
- The steps that caused the problem, if you can reproduce it.
- Your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version.
- Your Money Diary version, shown under **Settings > About**.
- A screenshot, if it helps explain the problem and does not reveal sensitive information.

Money Diary does not use an account system, analytics, or remote access to your app data. We cannot view or recover the records stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account.
